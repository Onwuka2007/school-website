Want to be a part of our staff?

At U-CEE School, we believe that education transforms lives. We're looking for passionate, dedicated, and innovative individualswho want to make a real differnce in the lives of students.

Why Work With Us?

  • A supportive and collaborative environment.
  • Opportunities for professional growth and training.
  • A chance to impact future leaders in our community.
  • A forward-thinking academic culture.

Here are the current available openings:

  • Subject Teachers (Arts, ICT)
  • Class Teachers (Primary 4, Nursery 2)
  • Administrative Staff
  • Non-Academic Staff

Requirements:

  • Minimum of [B.Ed / B.Sc / HND depending on role]
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Passion for education and student development.
  • Ability to work in a team and adapt to a dynamic school environment.

How to Apply:

Interested applicants should submit their details through the form below. After submitting, send their CV through the email provided. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted via email.

vacancy form

After submitting this form, please send your CV to careers@uceeschool.com

How we will use the information provided in this form

This information will be used by the School Management during the evaluation process in order to review your application and your suitability for a place at the school.

For more information about how the School will use your information, and your personal information, please contact us.
